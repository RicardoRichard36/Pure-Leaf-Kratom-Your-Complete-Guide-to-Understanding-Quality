## Storage and Freshness of Pure Leaf Kratom

Pure leaf kratom maintains potency longest when stored in cool, dry conditions away from direct sunlight. Proper storage prevents alkaloid degradation and maintains the natural properties that make pure leaf kratom appealing.

**Optimal storage conditions:**
- Temperature below 70°F when possible
- Humidity levels under 60%
- Airtight containers to prevent moisture exposure
- Dark storage areas away from UV light

Pure leaf kratom typically maintains full potency for 1-2 years when stored properly. Signs of degradation include color changes, musty odors, or clumping that indicates moisture exposure.

**Put simply:** Store kratom like you would store coffee or tea. Keep it sealed, cool, and dry for best results.
